Join Team Bagel!

Join our mission to bring New York bagel culture to the center of Bristol!

(F/T and P/T positions available)

All of our bagels are hand rolled, fermented for 18 hours and poached in the traditional way. All our coffee is sourced from local and quality focused roasteries.

This is the perfect role if you are passionate about trying new and delicious foods, you love great coffee and you have energy and enthusiasm to give great customer experiences!

What you'll be focused on:

  • • Customer experience: We will be a fast-paced takeaway focused business and so the small amount of time we spend with each customer is so important. Buying lunch out is a treat and every aspect from the person serving you to the moment your food touches your lips should be a joy. Help us create these experiences!
  • • Food: We operate with everyone being involved in everything. Baking, cooking, eating, discussing, we want everyone's ideas and opinions. Whether you are really experienced or yet to step into a commercial kitchen, we want to hear from you!
  • • Coffee: We are a coffee focused business. We have a set recipe for every drink, and work with passionate and high quality roasteries. If you have worked somewhere high paced where you never settle for anything but the perfect cup, you will fit right in. If not, we can get you there and love taking people on that journey.
  • • In an ideal world you will have experience in a bakery, as a barista or in a customer service role. In the real world however we’re banking on you having a can do attitude, charisma and enthusiasm.

Why you should work for us:

  • • A fun, energetic and exciting culture
  • • Four day working weeks (40 hours for full timers)
  • • Your rota 2+ weeks in advance
  • • Free lunch and paid break on shift, discount for friends or family and free coffee. Always
  • • Training on all areas including baking, barista-ing and ingredients
  • • Variety - the spice of life!
  • • Involvement in shaping the business as we grow
  • • Closed bank holidays and over Christmas. Obviously get standard holiday and an extra day for your birthday!
  • • Opportunities to go down multiple careers in the food and drink industry
  • • Bonuses for performance and length of service
  • • Always paying at least real living wage

Send an email with your fav bagel filling and why to: littlebagelcouk@gmail.com - no need for a CV (unless you want to send it!)
